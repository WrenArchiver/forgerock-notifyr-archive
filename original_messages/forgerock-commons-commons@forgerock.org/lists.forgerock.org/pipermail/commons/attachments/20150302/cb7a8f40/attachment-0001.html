IX,&nbsp;should&nbsp;fully&nbsp;describe&nbsp;all&nbsp;the&nbsp;arguments&nbsp;that&nbsp;you&nbsp;can&nbsp;provide&nbsp;to&nbsp;a&nbsp;command.&nbsp;\\&nbsp;Reference&nbsp;manual&nbsp;pages&nbsp;can&nbsp;also&nbsp;include&nbsp;supplementary&nbsp;material&nbsp;(extra&nbsp;descriptions,&nbsp;explanations&nbsp;about&nbsp;dependencies,&nbsp;examples,&nbsp;see&nbsp;also&nbsp;links&nbsp;to&nbsp;other&nbsp;documentation).&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;not&nbsp;clear&nbsp;that&nbsp;it&nbsp;makes&nbsp;sense&nbsp;to&nbsp;attach&nbsp;the&nbsp;supplementary&nbsp;information&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptor.<br />*&nbsp;GUIs&nbsp;might&nbsp;call&nbsp;for&nbsp;reference&nbsp;documentation&nbsp;concerning&nbsp;the&nbsp;values&nbsp;you&nbsp;can&nbsp;set&nbsp;in&nbsp;form&nbsp;input&nbsp;fields.&nbsp;\\&nbsp;Documentation&nbsp;for&nbsp;hand-written&nbsp;GUIs&nbsp;seems&nbsp;separate&nbsp;and&nbsp;outside&nbsp;the&nbsp;scope&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptor.<br /><br /><br />h3.&nbsp;Doc&nbsp;requirements&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptors<br /><br />What&nbsp;is&nbsp;needed&nbsp;depends&nbsp;on&nbsp;the&nbsp;type&nbsp;of&nbsp;documentation.<br /><br />h4.&nbsp;Requirements&nbsp;for&nbsp;API&nbsp;reference&nbsp;documentation<br /><br />API&nbsp;descriptors&nbsp;should&nbsp;be&nbsp;easy&nbsp;to&nbsp;find&nbsp;(well-known&nbsp;location&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;deployed&nbsp;product).<br /><br />API&nbsp;descriptors&nbsp;should&nbsp;reflect&nbsp;the&nbsp;concrete&nbsp;API&nbsp;at&nbsp;runtime.<br />It&nbsp;would&nbsp;be&nbsp;nice&nbsp;to&nbsp;have&nbsp;the&nbsp;default&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptors&nbsp;available&nbsp;without&nbsp;installing&nbsp;and&nbsp;configuring&nbsp;the&nbsp;service&nbsp;as&nbsp;well&nbsp;to&nbsp;build&nbsp;static&nbsp;docs&nbsp;for&nbsp;a&nbsp;product&nbsp;release.<br /><br />API&nbsp;descriptors&nbsp;should&nbsp;make&nbsp;it&nbsp;possible&nbsp;to&nbsp;derive&nbsp;the&nbsp;following&nbsp;for&nbsp;API&nbsp;documentation:<br /><br />*&nbsp;API&nbsp;type&nbsp;(CREST,&nbsp;HTTP,&nbsp;...)<br />*&nbsp;API&nbsp;version&nbsp;information<br />*&nbsp;API</span> <span class="diffaddedchars" style="background-color:#ddfade;">&nbsp;access&nbsp;(so&nbsp;the&nbsp;documentation&nbsp;can&nbsp;expose&nbsp;only&nbsp;what&nbsp;is&nbsp;earmarked&nbsp;for&nbsp;public,&nbsp;external&nbsp;use)<br />*&nbsp;API</span> <span class="diffcontext">&nbsp;description,&nbsp;potentially&nbsp;including&nbsp;markup<br />*&nbsp;Enumeration&nbsp;of&nbsp;all&nbsp;parameters&nbsp;of&nbsp;valid&nbsp;requests<br />**&nbsp;E.g.&nbsp;when&nbsp;HTTP&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;transport:&nbsp;paths,&nbsp;query&nbsp;string&nbsp;parameters,&nbsp;fragments,&nbsp;headers,&nbsp;entity&nbsp;(resource)<br />**&nbsp;Constraints&nbsp;on&nbsp;values<br />**&nbsp;Default&nbsp;values<br />**&nbsp;Example&nbsp;values<br />*&nbsp;Enumeration&nbsp;of&nbsp;all&nbsp;responses&nbsp;(both&nbsp;success&nbsp;and&nbsp;errors)<br />**&nbsp;E.g.&nbsp;when&nbsp;HTTP&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;transport:&nbsp;Status&nbsp;codes,&nbsp;headers,&nbsp;entity&nbsp;(resource)<br />*&nbsp;For&nbsp;each&nbsp;resource<br />**&nbsp;Format&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;resource&nbsp;(JSON,&nbsp;XML,&nbsp;etc.)<br />**&nbsp;Schema&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;resource<br />**&nbsp;Relationships&nbsp;to&nbsp;other&nbsp;resources<br />**&nbsp;What&nbsp;operations&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;performed&nbsp;on&nbsp;the&nbsp;resource<br />*&nbsp;Descriptions&nbsp;for&nbsp;all&nbsp;elements,&nbsp;potentially&nbsp;including&nbsp;markup<br /><br />*Notes*<br />*&nbsp;On&nbsp;the&nbsp;implementation&nbsp;side,&nbsp;use&nbsp;of&nbsp;conventions&nbsp;can&nbsp;prevent&nbsp;the&nbsp;need&nbsp;to&nbsp;maintain&nbsp;boilerplate.<br />*&nbsp;If&nbsp;we&nbsp;define&nbsp;an&nbsp;HTTP&nbsp;type,&nbsp;could&nbsp;we&nbsp;write&nbsp;the&nbsp;descriptor&nbsp;by&nbsp;hand&nbsp;for&nbsp;non-CREST&nbsp;APIs?<br />*&nbsp;How&nbsp;would&nbsp;one&nbsp;describe&nbsp;an&nbsp;API&nbsp;like&nbsp;OpenAM's&nbsp;/json/authenticate?<br />*&nbsp;Maybe&nbsp;the&nbsp;API&nbsp;descri
