&nbsp;the&nbsp;following&nbsp;high&nbsp;level&nbsp;requirements&nbsp;in&nbsp;order&nbsp;of&nbsp;priority:<br /><br />#&nbsp;integrate&nbsp;live&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;data&nbsp;in&nbsp;web&nbsp;applications:&nbsp;this&nbsp;will&nbsp;always&nbsp;require&nbsp;reference&nbsp;documentation.&nbsp;In&nbsp;practice,&nbsp;I&nbsp;think&nbsp;that&nbsp;individual&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;attributes&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;selected&nbsp;for&nbsp;display&nbsp;by&nbsp;developers&nbsp;rather&nbsp;than&nbsp;mechanically&nbsp;read&nbsp;and&nbsp;displayed&nbsp;(i.e.&nbsp;administrators&nbsp;will&nbsp;only&nbsp;be&nbsp;interested&nbsp;in&nbsp;what&nbsp;they&nbsp;consider&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;important&nbsp;statistics,&nbsp;not&nbsp;everything).&nbsp;Our&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;data&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;more&nbsp;&quot;consumable&quot;&nbsp;if&nbsp;it&nbsp;is&nbsp;exposed&nbsp;via&nbsp;a&nbsp;REST&nbsp;API<br />#&nbsp;a&nbsp;lightweight&nbsp;CLI&nbsp;for&nbsp;quickly&nbsp;deploying&nbsp;new&nbsp;instances.&nbsp;This&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;requirement&nbsp;for&nbsp;elasticity&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;cloud,&nbsp;increasing&nbsp;use&nbsp;of&nbsp;devops&nbsp;patterns&nbsp;like&nbsp;&quot;[Phoenix&nbsp;Server|http://martinfowler.com/bliki/PhoenixServer.html]&quot;,&nbsp;increasing&nbsp;use&nbsp;of&nbsp;tools&nbsp;like&nbsp;Puppet&nbsp;and&nbsp;Docker,&nbsp;etc.&nbsp;We&nbsp;already&nbsp;have&nbsp;this&nbsp;today&nbsp;with&nbsp;dsconfig&nbsp;over&nbsp;LDAP<br />#&nbsp;configuration&nbsp;reference&nbsp;guide&nbsp;and&nbsp;manual&nbsp;pages,&nbsp;although&nbsp;these&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;derived&nbsp;automatically&nbsp;from&nbsp;the&nbsp;CLI<br />#&nbsp;configuration&nbsp;UI&nbsp;-&nbsp;administrators&nbsp;generally&nbsp;configure&nbsp;OpenDJ&nbsp;using&nbsp;a&nbsp;CLI&nbsp;so&nbsp;I&nbsp;don't&nbsp;think&nbsp;this&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;high&nbsp;priority&nbsp;requirement.&nbsp;However,&nbsp;we'd&nbsp;get&nbsp;it&nbsp;for&nbsp;free&nbsp;(or&nbsp;very&nbsp;cheaply!)&nbsp;if&nbsp;we&nbsp;have&nbsp;sufficient&nbsp;tooling&nbsp;for&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptors<br />#&nbsp;server&nbsp;side&nbsp;developer&nbsp;tooling&nbsp;such&nbsp;as&nbsp;annotations&nbsp;support:&nbsp;we&nbsp;already&nbsp;have&nbsp;a&nbsp;powerful&nbsp;framework&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;OpenDJ&nbsp;configuration,&nbsp;but&nbsp;a&nbsp;modern&nbsp;annotation/binding&nbsp;based&nbsp;approach&nbsp;would&nbsp;be&nbsp;even&nbsp;more&nbsp;developer&nbsp;friendly.&nbsp;No&nbsp;such&nbsp;framework&nbsp;exists&nbsp;for&nbsp;developing&nbsp;new&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;and&nbsp;administrative&nbsp;tasks&nbsp;capabilities,&nbsp;so&nbsp;server&nbsp;side&nbsp;tooling&nbsp;would&nbsp;be&nbsp;of&nbsp;benefit<br />#&nbsp;maintain&nbsp;LDAP&nbsp;access&nbsp;to&nbsp;our&nbsp;configuration,&nbsp;monitoring,&nbsp;and&nbsp;tasks.&nbsp;It&nbsp;may&nbsp;be&nbsp;possible&nbsp;to&nbsp;switch&nbsp;our&nbsp;task&nbsp;support&nbsp;from&nbsp;LDAP&nbsp;to&nbsp;HTTP,&nbsp;but&nbsp;I&nbsp;think&nbsp;that&nbsp;doing&nbsp;something&nbsp;similar&nbsp;for&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;and&nbsp;configuration&nbsp;could&nbsp;have&nbsp;a&nbsp;large&nbsp;impact&nbsp;on&nbsp;existing&nbsp;users&nbsp;(TBC&nbsp;with&nbsp;Ludo).<br /><br />Now&nbsp;in&nbsp;more&nbsp;detail.<br /><br />h4.&nbsp;Must&nbsp;have&nbsp;support&nbsp;for:<br />#&nbsp;all&nbsp;the&nbsp;must-have&nbsp;requirements&nbsp;for&nbsp;Rest2LDAP<br />#&nbsp;if&nbsp;we&nbsp;want&nbsp;to&nbsp;expose&nbsp;our&nbsp;configuration&nbsp;over&nbsp;REST&nbsp;then&nbsp;the&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptor&nbsp;must&nbsp;be&nbsp;provide&nbsp;a&nbsp;similar&nbsp;level&nbsp;of&nbsp;functionality&nbsp;defined&nbsp;in&nbsp;our&nbsp;XML&nbsp;descriptor&nbsp;[schema|http://sources.forgerock.org/browse/opendj/trunk/opendj/opendj-maven-plugin/src/main/resources/config/stylesheets/admin.xsd?hb=true],&nbsp;which&nbsp;is&nbsp;currently&nbsp;used&nbsp;for&nbsp;describing&nbsp;components&nbsp;like&nbsp;our&nbsp;[LDAP&nbsp;connection&nbsp;handler|http://sources.forgerock.org/browse/opendj/trunk/opendj/opendj-maven-plugin/src/main/resources/config/xml/org/forgerock/opendj/server/config/LDAPConnectionHandlerConfiguration.xml?hb=true]&nbsp;and&nbsp;our&nbsp;[JE&nbsp;backend|http://sources.forgerock.org/browse/opendj/trunk/opendj/opendj-maven-plugin/src/main/resources/config/xml/org/forgerock/opendj/server/config/LocalDBBackendConfiguration.xml?r=11737].&nbsp;In&nbsp;addition&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;Rest2LDAP&nbsp;requirements&nbsp;we&nbsp;will&nbsp;need&nbsp;to&nbsp;express:<br />#*&nbsp;administration&nbsp;related&nbsp;hints,&nbsp;such&nbsp;as&nbsp;whether&nbsp;a&nbsp;component&nbsp;will&nbsp;need&nbsp;the&nbsp;server&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;restarted&nbsp;for&nbsp;changes&nbsp;to&nbsp;take&nbsp;effect<br />#*&nbsp;default&nbsp;behaviors,&nbsp;including&nbsp;whether&nbsp;a&nbsp;property&nbsp;has&nbsp;a&nbsp;default&nbsp;value,&nbsp;whether&nbsp;it&nbsp;inherits&nbsp;default&nbsp;values&nbsp;from&nbsp;somewhere&nbsp;else,&nbsp;etc<br />#*&nbsp;constraints&nbsp;between&nbsp;properties&nbsp;and&nbsp;components:&nbsp;for&nbsp;example,&nbsp;if&nbsp;the&nbsp;LDAP&nbsp;listener&nbsp;has&nbsp;SSL&nbsp;enabled&nbsp;or&nbsp;StartTLS&nbsp;enabled,&nbsp;then&nbsp;the&nbsp;&quot;key&nbsp;manager&quot;&nbsp;property&nbsp;must&nbsp;reference&nbsp;a&nbsp;key&nbsp;manager&nbsp;which&nbsp;is&nbsp;also&nbsp;enabled.&nbsp;OpenDJ's&nbsp;mechanism&nbsp;using&nbsp;an&nbsp;XML&nbsp;based&nbsp;condition&nbsp;language&nbsp;is&nbsp;possibly&nbsp;a&nbsp;bit&nbsp;over-engineered.&nbsp;It&nbsp;may&nbsp;be&nbsp;more&nbsp;flexible&nbsp;if&nbsp;conditions&nbsp;are&nbsp;included&nbsp;in&nbsp;API&nbsp;descriptors&nbsp;<br />#&nbsp;server&nbsp;side&nbsp;developer&nbsp;tooling&nbsp;for&nbsp;monitoring&nbsp;and&nbsp;task&nbsp;support:&nbsp;an&nbsp;annotation&nbsp;based&nbsp;binding&nbsp;layer&nbsp;would&nbsp;seem&nbsp;like&nbsp;a&nbsp;good&nbsp;fit.&nbsp;This&nbsp;is&nbsp;must&nbsp;have&nbsp;because&nbsp;it&nbsp;provides&nbsp;a&nbsp;single&nbsp;point&nbsp;of&nbsp;reference&nbsp;between&nbsp;the&nbsp;server-side&nbsp;developers&nbsp;and&nb
