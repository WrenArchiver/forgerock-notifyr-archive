b410fa1fe9a DOCS-379 Prevent links to arbitrary resources in PDF

This patch prevents links to arbitrary resources in PDF.

Although the links in the PDF are technically correct,
they do not seem to work in browser-based viewers,
at least not in Safari and Firefox on Mac OS X.
As a result it seems safer to prevent the links from breaking
by removing links to arbitrary resources for this format.

A more long-term fix could be to edit the links
to point to the online resources (see DOCS-387).
The location for the online resources depends on the project,
and on whether the docs are draft or release versions.		 16 Feb 2016
