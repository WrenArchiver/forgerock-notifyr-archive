a8fd21bfb0f CHF-80 Guard against any kind of Throwable that may be thrown synchronously

Any `RuntimeException` thrown by Handlers/Filters before returning their Promise
(i.e. not caught by Promise `RuntimeExceptionHandler`), are flowing directly into
the web container, possibly leaving a stale response (was observed with Tomcat 8) :'(

Servlet specification indicates that it's the responsibility of the Servlet implementer
to call `AsyncContext.complete()`

This change-set catch all of them and force `AsyncContext` completion:
* In case of synchronous `Throwable`, generate a `500` status
* Refactor `writeResponse()` to avoid code duplication		 23 Sep 2015
