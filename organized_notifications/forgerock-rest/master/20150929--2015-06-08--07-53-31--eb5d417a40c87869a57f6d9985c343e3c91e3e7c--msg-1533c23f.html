eb5d417a40c CREST-165 Clarify paged results contract

This commit aims to solidify the paged query contract. Offsets and cookies
are now mutually exclusive. Cookies are only guaranteed in the response of
filter-based queries. Cookies may be supported for other query methods
(queryId/queryExpression) but there is no guarantee.		 08 Jun 2015
